Our History

The Beginning

After the dissolution of Team Hotcakes, Omrii and Hales wanted to continue their Counter-Strike legacy. After numerous failed attempts at finding a new home on the infamous CEVO Forums, They've talked things over and thus, thank mr friberg was born.
Initialy the roster was composed of:

  • Omrii
  • Hales
  • b1ur
  • bo4r
  • Dingus

And together as a team, the first Thank Mr Friberg line-up went to CEVO's 8th season.

CEVO Season Eight

They've constructed a practice time schedule, set up strats for each map and played every once in a while (on top of practice, of course).

After they've played all of their games, they did... Okay-ish.
Rank 88 out of like 1123 teams. Just short of the 64th spot though :\ (which was required to continue to the play-offs).

Off-Season

After the season ended, Victor and bo4r left. They liked TF2 more, and CS:GO just wasn't their cup of tea.

After a lot of scowering, DiZzy and TuxdedoWolf.

  • Omrii
  • Hales
  • b1ur
  • TuxedoWolf
  • DiZzy

They've scrimmed a lot and stuff but DiZzy and TuxedoWolf were like "fuck these guys, lets just make our team". And they did just that. And left thank mr friberg left for dead.

Omrii and Hales managed to get Rebel and Nate onboard, the best IGL and AWPer in the world (except for b1ur, of course).
